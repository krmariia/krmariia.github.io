My first Camera

I was 5 years old, when I took photocamera in my hands for the first time. My family, as well as others at that time, had a film camera. Latter, we bought one more - simple Kodak for average users. Average camera - amateur photos. But when I turned fifteen, my parents bought me Canon 600D... my first professional camera, which I am using nowadays as well.

Experience

To begin with, I have never visited any photo courses, exept the required one - Video Production at my university. Since I received the present (Cannon 600D), I started to experiment and thus gradually learned everything I know on my own. I am still not a professional, nevertheless I am able to work in Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop and DaVicni Resolve, I continue learning, as there is always place for self-development.Having camera is not enough, you need to have willing to do what you like and developed imagination, to notice what others cannot see. However, I am photoshoting only 2 years, I already got a chance to participate in several exebitions in my hometown.
